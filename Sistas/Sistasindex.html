SISTAs CanSurvive Coalition, Inc.

Who Are We

Mission

Our mission is to increase breast, cervical, ovarian and prostate cancer survivorship and quality of life of those affected by the disease. This includes services providing cost free exercise and nutrition aftercare, support groups and research.

About Us

SCSC is a 501(c)3, non-profit grassroots organization providing culturally sensitive focus to combat and cope with the disease of breast, cervical, ovarian, prostate and other related conditions among the medically underserved.

Our goal is to provide emotional support to survivors and their families, volunteers, and other individuals. These persons may reside in Montgomery, rural areas of Alabama, or other states. The Survivors may be survivors of cancer or other chronic illnesses.
